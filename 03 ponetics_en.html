This section focuses on the phonetic and phonological aspect of Lamo language, including consonantal and vowel inventories, as well as some specific phonological characteristics and constrasts, such as prenasals and prefricatives and vowel tense-lax contrast. At the end of this section, a representative word will be presented for each phoneme of the language.

Consonants

Two varieties of the Lamo language, Lamo and Lamei, share the identical consonantal Inventory.

The Consonantal Inventory of Lamo Language
Bilabial Dental Alveolar Postalveolar Retroflex Palatal Velar Uvular Glottal
Plosive p pʰ b t tʰ d ʈ ɖ k kʰ ɡ
Nasal m n ɲ ŋ ɴ
Fricative s z ɕ ʂ ʐ ç x ɣ h
Affricates t͡s t͡sʰ d͡z t͡ɕ t͡ɕʰ d͡ʑ ʈ͡ʂ ʈ͡ʂʰ ɖ͡ʐ
Lateral Fricative ɬ
Approximant w j
Lateral Approximant l
